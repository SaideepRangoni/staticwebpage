Our Ahar Setu Project

Welcome to our Ahar Setu Project, where we connect food donors with those in need. Our mission is to reduce food wastage while alleviating hunger in our communities.

Why Food Donation Matters

Every year, millions of tons of food are wasted, while many people go hungry. By donating food, you help make a difference in someone's life and contribute to a more sustainable future.

How It Works

  1. Donors can post available food items on our platform.
  2. Beneficiaries can browse available donations by location.
  3. Once a beneficiary claims a post, they receive a confirmation email.
  4. Donated food is picked up and delivered to those in need.
