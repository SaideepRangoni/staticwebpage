About Us

Our Mission

To connect restaurants willing to donate excess food with orphanages and individuals facing food insecurity.

Our Vision

To foster a community where surplus food is shared, ensuring access to nutritious meals for everyone.

Our Goals

  • Reduce food wastage from restaurants.
  • Provide an efficient platform for food donation.
  • Support those in need with essential supplies.
  • Raise awareness about food waste.

How It Works

  1. Restaurants post available food items.
  2. Beneficiaries browse listings by location.
  3. Food is collected and distributed safely.
